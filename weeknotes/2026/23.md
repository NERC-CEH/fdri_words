## AWS Migration

Not loads left to do on the migration so been a slightly quieter week, but lots going on in the background. We are getting ready to migrate the Phonocam ingestion service (once everything is setup in the database), we have been getting the infrastructure setup to allow Flux data to be ingested into the new accounts and we have been discussing the best way forward to switching the sensors over to the new accounts (and how we test this).

## Data API

In preperation for the soft launch in September we have been adapting our API to restrict certain endpoints (Phenocam and NRFA) and networks (NRFA and others) for users outside of UKCEH. As an intermin solution whilst longer-term company wide solutions are reviewed, we are restricting access based on the IP address of the user.
