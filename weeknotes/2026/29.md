## Time Series Data Explorer Redesign
<img width="1918" height="973" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c484ab2f-08e7-4e76-ba02-9ed5c95d6683" />

Our soon to be public time series data explorer got a bit of a redesign this week. See it here: [https://dri-ui.dri.ceh.ac.uk/fdri/timeseries](https://dri-ui.dri.ceh.ac.uk/fdri/timeseries)

The design is roughly based on our landing page and we added a few more features:
- Log scaling improved in the explore chart to avoid large empty spaces appearing in the plots. 
- Zoom features in plots improved for a smoother user experience.
- More empty state content
- subtle animations across the board
- csv download on plots
- footer and scrolling of charts rework
- horizontal view fixed
- shows raw/processed in the variable list
- share pop over
- combobox search for network/variable reworked



### Learn About and Data Story pages
We have added Learn About and Data Story pages based on the work done by David Green.  They are working, but only have placeholder content at the moment whilst the real content is written.  The Learn About pages provide context and guidance on how to use the tool (eg Timeseries and Spatial Explorers) whilst the Data Story pages show how they are used in real scenarios showing off their features by populating the tool itself or images of it during use.  The links to these pages should be ready in time for the next edition of weeknotes so you can try them out.
